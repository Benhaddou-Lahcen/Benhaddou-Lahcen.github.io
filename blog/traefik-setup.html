Setting Up Traefik with Docker & Let's Encrypt

Published on November 14, 2025

Traefik is a modern reverse proxy that makes exposing services easy with automatic SSL via Let's Encrypt. Here's how to set it up in production.

1. Docker Compose Structure

traefik:
  image: traefik:v2.10
  ports: [80, 443]
  volumes: ['./traefik.yml:/etc/traefik/traefik.yml', './acme.json:/acme.json']

Add your services with labels, and Traefik auto-discovers them.
